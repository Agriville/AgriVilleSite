Welcome to AgriVille!

Agriville is a minimalist simple farming game that has fun and engaging gameplay. In this game, players are tasked with managing their own virtual farm, planting and harvesting crops, and raising animals. One of the things that sets Agriville apart from other farming games is its focus on simplicity. The gameplay is straightforward and easy to pick up, making it accessible to players of all ages and skill levels. The graphics are also kept minimalistic, with a clean and colorful interface that adds to the game's charm. Despite its simplicity, Agriville offers a lot of depth and replay value. Players can choose from a variety of crops and animals to raise, and each has its own unique characteristics and challenges. For example, some crops may grow faster or yield more rewards, while certain animals may require more care and attention.
